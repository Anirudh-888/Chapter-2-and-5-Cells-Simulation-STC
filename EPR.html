Key Biology Facts

The ER membrane is continuous with the outer nuclear membrane, forming one interconnected endomembrane system.

The Rough ER gets its bumpy look from ribosomes dotted on its cytoplasmic face.

The Smooth ER is tubular, produces lipids, steroid hormones, and helps liver cells break down drugs.

Together, both types account for more than half of a cell's total membrane area.
